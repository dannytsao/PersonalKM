# Codex

OpenAI's Codex AI coding agent was discovered to have a serious bug causing excessive background SSD writes, reportedly accumulating ~37TB of writes in just 21 days. At this rate, SSD write endurance (warranty lifespan) could be exhausted in less than a year, posing significant risk for heavy users

## Key Facts

- Codex bug causes ~37TB of SSD writes within 21 days of background operation
- SSD write endurance could be exhausted in less than a year under this rate
- Article provides a self-check method to detect excessive SSD writes
- Impact is most severe for users running Codex continuously for coding or remote operation
