## Summary

Ruilong Waterfall Park (瑞龍瀑布園區) is a family-friendly nature attraction located in Zhushan Township, Nantou County, Taiwan. The trail is relatively flat with well-maintained facilities, taking about 40 minutes one-way and roughly one hour for a complete loop through the forested area. Along the route

## Key Facts

- Location: Zhushan Township, Nantou County, Taiwan (南投縣竹山鎮)
- One-way trail takes approximately 40 minutes to walk
- Full loop through the forest park takes about 1 hour
- Features waterfalls and a suspension bridge along the trail
- Trail is gentle/low elevation change, suitable for families with children
